MIND THAT WORD

Mind That Word is basically a game in which there will be a team of two players, one has to guess the word given to the other by the game-master, the game will be played over LAN. The player has to describe the word to his teammate without using the set of five taboo words which will be given with the word.Any use of encoding to disclose the word will be counted as a foul and the team will be debarred for that round.
