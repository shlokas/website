IPL AUCTION

IPL Auction is a virtual managing event where participants build their team by bidding for the players they want. The event will be organized in 3 slots and a *SLOT OF CHAMPIONS*. But be careful and smart! Each player has credits assigned to them. Interesting twists await you here! Get your strategies right and claim additional bonus! Two teams having maximum credits at the end of the slot qualifies for SLOT OF CHAMPIONS.
